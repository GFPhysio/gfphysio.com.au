Cricket

THE ROCKET
FACTORY

Georgia recently joined The Rocket Factory — cricket's elite fast bowling development program. Working alongside specialist coaches and high performance staff, Georgia's role is to keep the next generation of pace bowlers healthy, resilient, and available to bowl.


Fast bowling is one of the most physically demanding actions in sport, placing enormous stress on the spine, hips, and lower limbs. Georgia's work focuses on load management, bowling-specific rehabilitation, and building the physical foundations for long, high-performance careers.

Before becoming a physiotherapist, Georgia competed as a semi-professional basketball player. This background gives her something most clinicians can't offer — a genuine understanding of what it means to train hard, compete, get injured, and fight to get back.


When Georgia talks about the frustration of injury, or the pressure to return to play, she's not just speaking clinically — she's speaking from lived experience. That perspective shapes everything about how she works with athletes and active people.
